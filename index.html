Privacy Policy for Bravo Blog

At Bravo Blog, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the information related to your use of our Shopify app.

Information We Collect:

1.1 Blog Post Backup:

In order to provide our backup function, we store a copy of the blog posts you create or edit using our app. This data is securely stored on our servers and is used solely for the purpose of enabling you to restore your blog posts if needed.

1.2 No Customer Data Collection:

We do not collect any customer data from your Shopify store. Our app does not have access to or store any information related to your customers, such as their names, email addresses, purchase history, or any other personal data.

How We Use Your Information:

2.1 Blog Post Backup:

The blog post data we store is used exclusively for the backup and restoration functionality of our app. We do not use this data for any other purposes, such as analysis, marketing, or sharing with third parties.

2.2 No Customer Data Usage:

As we do not collect any customer data, we do not use or process any information related to your store's customers.

Data Security:

3.1

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect the blog post data stored on our servers. This includes the use of encryption, firewalls, and secure access controls to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or modification of your data.

3.2

Despite our best efforts, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, while we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Third-Party Services:

4.1

We do not share your blog post data with any third-party services or partners, except for the necessary infrastructure required to provide our app's functionality (e.g., Heroku, AWS).

Changes to This Privacy Policy:

5.1

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the revised Privacy Policy within our app or on our website.

5.2

It is your responsibility to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Your continued use of our app after any modifications to the Privacy Policy will signify your acceptance of the updated terms.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at support@Bravoblogapp.com.
